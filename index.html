DRAGON SHIFT

Embark on an epic adventure with DragonShift, a unique turn-based Fantasy-Adventure-RPG with hundreds of dungeons to explore, lots of puzzles, decision-driven campaigns and fun online modes that let you measure your skills with players from around the world.

DragonShift combines:

  • The classic Fantasy-RPG experience
  • The shifting world map that spawns a new sub-genre in puzzle-RPG games
  • The unrepeatability of Chess
  • A sweet mix of Strategy and Luck
Read More

The world of DragonShift is a vast realm with grid-like dungeons filled with blocks, each of them hosting various game entities relevant to the gameplay. But what makes DragonShift truly unique is its shape-shifting dungeons, where you can move blocks around just like in a classic sliding puzzle. This means that every time you enter a dungeon, it's a new experience that will keep you guessing and strategizing every step of the way.

Choose from 4 competitive game modes, each with their own set of rules and challenges

  • Dragon's Blood is the standard mode, where each level can have its own rules, with the Classic levels usually requiring you to either Escape or Eliminate others: you escape by collecting certain items (often the map, key, torch) and reaching an escape spot (which is usually guarded by a Dragon!); you eliminate other players by acquiring the 'Dragon's Blood', which allows you to perma-kill any character.
  • In Sharpest Hour, the pace is faster and the leveling up is easier, making it perfect for a more casual style of play.
  • Cutthroat is all about eliminating other players, with no respawning and only one goal in mind, match duration being even shorter than in Sharpest Hour.
  • And in Darkest Hour, the Darkness Fog slowly devours everything in an area that grows with each passing day, giving you a limited amount of time to escape or eliminate others

But that's not all - DragonShift also has an Adventure mode that takes you on a journey across hundreds of levels, each with its own unique challenges and rewards. Your decisions matter in this mode, so choose wisely and unlock rewards that can be used in competitive mode.

Whether you're playing solo or teaming up with others, DragonShift is an exciting and fun adventure that will keep you coming back for more!

ONLINE

Test your skills against real players in Online mode: join with or against other warriors of similar ranks and solve some of the toughest dungeons! Earn Dragon Scales or Orbs that you can spend on goodies, Vault Items of different rarities that you can trade, sell or just flex about. Climb the skill ladder to unlock amazing and sometimes one-of-a-kind rewards! Join the DragonShift community to stay in touch or follow us on Twitter to be the first to find out about giveaways and new content.

TRAINING

Want to have an edge over other players? Fine-tune your skills Training mode where you can explore any dungeon by yourself with no time limit and useful tips, and brew a mind-blowing strategy; or, even cooler, find exploits (before we fix them!).
